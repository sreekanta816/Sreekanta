Trophy

In cricket, **trophies** symbolize **victory, achievement, and excellence**. They are awarded to teams or individuals for winning matches, tournaments, or achieving special milestones. Trophies like the **ICC World Cup**, **Ashes**, and **IPL trophy** are prestigious and highly sought-after. They serve as **motivation** for players and represent **honor and recognition** at both national and international levels. Individual trophies are also awarded for **best batsman**, **bowler**, or **player of the match/series**. Displayed in cabinets or presentations, trophies foster team spirit, sportsmanship, and legacy. They are cherished memorabilia, reminding players and fans of **glorious performances and historic moments** in cricket.
